## Variant Style Specification
Each question includes 10 stylized answers for diverse training/retrieval scenarios:
1.  **Simple**: Direct and concise.
2.  **Interview**: Professional and competency-focused.
3.  **Technical**: Mathematically rigorous.
4.  **Exam**: Curriculum-aligned summary.
5.  **Analogy**: Simplified conceptual comparison.
6.  **One-liner**: Minimum character count.
7.  **Deep Explanation**: Exhaustive architectural/theoretical context.
8.  **Beginner Friendly**: Jargon-free conceptualization.
9.  **Advanced**: Implementation specific & hardware considerations.
10. **Strict Definition**: Formal lexicographical style.
